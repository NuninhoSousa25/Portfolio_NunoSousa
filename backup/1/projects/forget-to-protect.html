Forget to Protect: Memory & Preservation

2024 Wax Sculptures / Installation

A collaboration with Sofia Aires resulting in a series of wax sculptures that explore themes of memory, vulnerability, and the act (or failure) of preservation. The ephemeral nature of wax itself becomes part of the commentary.

Project Details

  • Collaboration: w/ Sofia Aires
  • Medium: Wax Sculptures
  • Techniques: Wax Casting, Sculpting, Installation
  • Residency: Ectopia Lab, Lisboa (Feb 2024)
  • Exhibition: Metamorphosis, Ectopia Lab, Lisboa (Oct-Dec 2024)
