LIMBO: Sea Level Immersive Experience

2021 Live Audiovisual Performance

A live sound and visual performance created with André Araujo, reflecting on the environmental issue of rising sea levels and its impact. The work uses improvisation and synesthetic connections between sound and visuals to create a meditative and immersive experience.

This collaborative piece explores the relationship between environmental crisis and human consciousness through real-time audiovisual synthesis, creating an atmospheric journey that connects the audience with the subtle but persistent threat of climate change.

Project Details

  • Collaboration: w/ André Araujo (Sound & Visuals)
  • Techniques: Live Visuals (VJing), Live Sound Performance, Improvisation, Immersive Art
  • Performances (Selection):
    • Penumbra: O Banal que nos Escapa, Museu Santa Joana, Aveiro (Oct 2021)
    • MTF - Music Tech Fest, Aveiro (Oct 2021)
  • Related Publication: ISEA 2022 Paper - "LIMBO (2021) - The Sea Levels in a Sound and Visual Immersive Experience"
  • Technology: Real-time audiovisual synthesis, Live improvisation systems
