Digital Debris: Fragments of Virtual Memory

2022 Video Installation

A video installation exploring the concept of digital memory and the fragments of virtual existence that accumulate in our digital landscape. Through images disturbed by competing content, memories merge into dreams, creating a space where entropy cohabits with recollection.

Project Details

  • Software Used: After Effects, Premiere Pro, Processing
  • Techniques: Video Art, Generative Processes, Multi-channel Installation
  • Exhibition: Depressa vai o tempo que vem, Espaço Porta-Jazz, Porto (Oct-Dec 2022)
