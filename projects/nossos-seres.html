Os Nossos Seres São Iguais: Identity & Existence

2022-2023 Multimedia Installation / Performance

A multifaceted project ('Our beings are alike') developed with André Araujo, exploring themes of identity, memory, and shared existence through digital prints, video installation, and live performance. It often draws inspiration from specific locations and archives.

Project Details

  • Collaboration: w/ André Araujo
  • Media: Digital Print in Textile (3.20x1.36m), Video Installation, Live Performance
  • Techniques: Audiovisual Performance, Digital Art, Installation Art
  • Exhibitions/Performances (Selection):
    • Reimaginados, Ectopia Lab, Lisbon (Nov 2022 - Feb 2023)
    • Ecos, Faculty of Fine Arts Gallery, Lisbon (Feb 2023)
    • Prespectivas, Odemira (Aug 2022)
    • Subtilesas, Cultivamos Cultura, São Luís (Aug 2022)
    • Montras, Cultivamos Cultura, São Luís (Aug 2022)
